Real roads, no run-off, riders topping 300 km/h between stone walls and hedgerows. I want to stand trackside and feel that kind of adrenaline up close, not just watch it on a screen.

Edinburg Scotland

Edinburgh is my favorite place to visit because of its magical blend of rich history, stunning natural landscapes, and incredible architecture. From wandering the medieval, cobblestone streets of the Old Town to hiking up Arthur’s Seat for breathtaking coastal views, the city offers an unmatched atmospheric charm. It is a place where vibrant, modern culture and centuries of historic beauty come together perfectly.
